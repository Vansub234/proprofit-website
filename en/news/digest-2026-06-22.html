Despite a few minor bugs after the update, the team is actively working on fixes and promptly resolving any issues that arise.

  • 💳 BiniBIT crypto cards

According to available information, work on these products is in full swing, and releases may happen very soon.

For our team, BiniBIT remains the main project in the portfolio, which we continue to actively develop and support.
