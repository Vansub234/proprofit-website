🚀 BiniBIT launched a Telegram Mini App and keeps expanding its ecosystem

👤 ProProfit Team · 🗓️ June 18, 2026 · News · BiniBIT

It looks like the BiniBIT team isn't slowing down. After launching its own BiniChain network, users now have access to a full Telegram Mini App that brings together mining, staking, tasks, a referral system, a DEX and even launching your own tokens right inside Telegram. 🔥

According to the screenshots, already available

  • ✅ Staking up to 160% APR
  • ✅ Built-in crystal mining 💎
  • ✅ Converting crystals into the internal stablecoin USBI
  • ✅ Its own DEX platform BaiDEX
  • ✅ Creating and launching your own meme tokens 🚀
  • ✅ A 3-level referral program (20% / 10% / 5%)
  • ✅ A system of levels, boosts and daily tasks

What's especially interesting is how all products are integrated into a single ecosystem. The user stays active in the app, earns crystals, uses them inside the platform and interacts with BiniChain products without needing to install separate apps.

📈 What stands out

  • The team is accelerating product launches.
  • Its own DEX and token-launch tools have appeared.
  • The ecosystem is gradually closing in within Telegram.
  • User-retention mechanics are actively developing via tasks, levels and staking.

Personally, BiniBIT remains one of the most interesting projects in my portfolio. The coin has already shown serious growth, and the number of launched products keeps increasing.
