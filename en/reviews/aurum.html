AURUM Foundation is a next-generation fintech ecosystem that brings AI trading bots, tokenized gold (XAU), a Web3 neobank with cards and its own AURUM token together on a single platform. The project's idea is simple and powerful: give everyday users access to tools that used to be available only to professional traders and large funds. Below we break down what the ecosystem includes and how it works.

🎬 AURUM Foundation presentation in Russian

What is AURUM Foundation

AURUM positions itself as an ecosystem built on artificial intelligence and blockchain. At its core is the combination of "capital growth + technology + real assets." The main products are:

  • AI trading bots (EX-AI Bot, EX AI PRO, Zeus AI Bot) — automated 24/7 trading: the algorithms look for market opportunities and adapt their strategy on their own.
  • XAU gold packages — tokenized gold as a real-world asset (RWA). A 50/50 model: part in gold instruments, part in AI trading.
  • Web3 NeoBank — a crypto/fiat wallet, virtual and physical cards with Apple Pay / Google Pay support, and cashback.
  • Flash Loans — arbitrage tools for experienced users.
  • The AURUM token — used to activate services, for staking and for the burn mechanism.
  • Marketing plan — an affiliate program: you can build income by inviting other participants into the ecosystem.

XAU gold packages — the project's signature feature

The most interesting thing about AURUM is the pairing of digital technology with real gold. The gold packages work on a 50/50 model: half of the funds go into gold instruments (a defensive asset that has historically held its value), and half into AI trading (growth potential). There are several plans for different budgets, each with a holding period and the option to ultimately receive physical 99.99% gold (stored in Switzerland) or an NFT certificate for it.

How to start

Registration happens through a personal dashboard (back office), after which you choose a product — an AI bot, a gold package or working with the neobank card. For beginners it makes sense to start small, learn the interface and the products, and only then scale up.

What to keep in mind (DYOR)

As with any fintech project, approach it with a cool head:

  • Start with a small amount and test deposits and withdrawals before working with large sums.
  • Study the terms of the gold packages: holding period, exit conditions, fees.
  • High advertised returns always go hand in hand with elevated risk — allocate your capital sensibly.
  • Enable 2FA and keep your access credentials secure.
  • Affiliate income depends on your activity and on building your structure — it's work, not a "money button."

Bottom line

AURUM Foundation is an ambitious ecosystem that gathers trending directions under one roof: AI trading, tokenized gold and Web3 banking with cards. For those who believe in the combination of "technology + real assets" and are willing to dig in, the project offers a broad toolkit and a clear affiliate program. As everywhere in crypto and fintech — study the product, start carefully and make your own decisions.
