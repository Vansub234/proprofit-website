MetaBox is a digital online-learning platform built for the practical mastery of AI tools. The core idea: not scattered courses across the internet, but a unified system of "learning → practice → earning". Inside there are 8 tracks:

  • New blogging — how to create and promote content with the help of AI.

  • Midjourney — professional-grade image generation.
  • Nano Banana — modern generation tools.
  • AI photographer — creating photorealistic content.
  • AI Avatar — digital avatars for content and business.

Demand for AI skills is growing explosively: neural networks are used in marketing, design, content and business.
